Questions

What to expect.

Is my care really confidential?

Yes. Intake is private, communication is discreet, and your information is protected under HIPAA. Many of our patients are professionals who need privacy — we're built for it.

How fast can I start Medication-Assisted Recovery?

Often within the same week when clinically appropriate. After a confidential consult, your physician determines the right plan — including whether a home or in-office Suboxone induction fits — and we support you closely through the first days.

Do you take insurance?

The Recovery line works with insurance, Medicare, and self-pay options. The Concierge optimization membership is a subscription. We'll walk you through costs transparently at your consult. Exact pricing confirmed at consult.

What is the Concierge membership?

A subscription that gives you hormone optimization, medically-supported weight loss, physical exams, medication management, and 24/7 direct access to your provider — concierge medicine focused on optimization, not just treatment.

Can I be seen by telehealth?

Yes — much of our care is available virtually across Illinois, with in-person visits at our Park Ridge office when needed.
