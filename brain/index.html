What is a memory layer?

One source of truth that every AI tool can read from and write to

A personal memory layer is a knowledge store you own that sits between you and the tools you use every day. Your facts, decisions, preferences and project context get saved once — then surfaced to whatever tool you're working in.

Instead of re-explaining yourself in every new chat, your second brain remembers. It's the connective tissue between ChatGPT, Claude, Cursor, your notes and your devices, so your knowledge follows you everywhere.

Kudbee's approach is hands-on teaching plus consulting — not a hosted black box. You finish understanding exactly how it works, with the keys in your hands.

The problem

Every new chat starts from zero

Your knowledge is scattered across a dozen tools, and none of them talk to each other. The cost adds up fast.

✗ Knowledge is fragmented

Notes in one app, threads in another, decisions buried in chat history. Nothing is a single source of truth.

✗ You repeat yourself

The same prompts, the same background, typed again and again — slowing every piece of work you do.

FAQ

Questions, answered

What exactly is a personal memory layer or second brain?
It's a knowledge store you own that sits between you and the AI tools you use. Instead of every chat starting from zero, your facts, decisions, preferences and project context are saved once and surfaced to whatever tool you're working in — ChatGPT, Claude, Cursor, or your phone.
Do I have to be technical to build my own brain?
No. We meet you at your level. Non-technical individuals get a guided, low-code setup; technical teams get the architecture, patterns and integration depth to extend it. Either way you finish understanding how it works.
Does this lock me into a single AI vendor or platform?
No — that's the whole point. Your memory layer is yours and portable. It works across any platform or device, and if a tool changes or you switch providers, your knowledge comes with you.
Who owns and controls the data?
You do. This is hands-on teaching and consulting, not a hosted black box. You keep control of your data and the keys to it, and you understand every part of the system we build together.
How is this different from just using ChatGPT memory or notes apps?
Built-in tool memory is siloed inside that one tool and you don't control it. A notes app doesn't feed your AI automatically. Your own memory layer is the connective tissue: one portable source of truth that every tool can read from and write to.
How much does it cost and how do we start?
Scope and pricing are set on a free discovery call, because it depends on whether it's for an individual or a team and how deep you want to go. Book a call and we'll map your setup and propose a plan.
