◆ Support Agent Suite

Support Agent Suite

AI agents that organize customer requests, draft replies, and keep support moving — with humans still in control.

View Agent Capabilities

Built for customer support first. Expandable for the rest of the business.

The Problem

Support chaos is expensive

Messages pile up across channels, details slip, and the same replies get retyped all day. Most small teams can't justify a heavyweight support platform — but the work doesn't stop.

Messages come from everywhere

Email, chat, forms, DMs, and voicemails all land in different places — with no single view of what's open.

Details get missed

When threads run long and the queue is busy, important context and follow-ups quietly fall through the cracks.

Repetitive replies waste hours

The same questions get answered over and over, eating time that should go toward real work and real customers.

Enterprise tools cost too much

Full-scale support suites are priced for big teams. Small businesses need something lean that just works.

Owners need to move faster

Operators want to respond quickly and stay organized — without hiring a full support team to do it.

The Solution

One suite that keeps support moving

The Support Agent Suite reads what comes in, organizes it, and prepares the work — so a human can review, approve, and send in a fraction of the time.

  • Reads incoming support requests across channels
  • Categorizes each request by urgency and topic
  • Drafts professional replies for human review
  • Creates follow-up tasks so nothing is forgotten
  • Summarizes customer history into quick context
  • Flags risky or sensitive issues for a person to handle
  • Keeps the owner or operator in control at every step
Agent Capabilities

Your core support agents

Six focused agents that each own one part of the support workflow — working together, all reviewable by you.

Inbox Triage Agent

Sorts every incoming request by urgency and topic, so the queue is organized the moment messages arrive.

Reply Draft Agent

Drafts clear, professional responses in your voice — ready for a human to review, tweak, and approve.

Follow-Up Agent

Tracks what still needs action and reminds the team, so open items don't slip after the first reply.

Knowledge Base Agent

Turns repeated questions into reusable, on-brand answers so common requests get handled instantly.

Escalation Agent

Flags angry, legal, billing, or otherwise sensitive messages so a person reviews them before anything goes out.

Summary Agent

Condenses long back-and-forth threads into quick notes so anyone can pick up the context in seconds.

Human in the Loop

Agents assist. Humans approve.

The suite is built to speed up your team — not to act on its own. You stay the decision-maker on everything that matters.

  • No blind auto-sending by default — replies wait for review
  • The business owner controls every final decision
  • Sensitive issues are flagged for a human, not actioned automatically
  • Every agent output is reviewable before it's used
  • Permissions and access stay narrow by design

What we believe

The Kudbee Doctrine

Not a mission statement. A set of refusals — the things we won't compromise on when we build.

Most studios sell hours. We sell judgment. This is how we decide what to build, what to skip, and what we'll never ship — written down so you can hold us to it.

Living document · Last revised June 2026

Article I

Make it real, not a render

Anyone can screenshot an AI demo. We'd rather ship the working thing — the concierge on our home page is real UI you can talk to, not a mockup, and it goes fully live the moment its Worker backend ships. If we show it, you can touch it. Slideware is for people who can't build.

Article II

Own your stack

We build on infrastructure you can actually keep: static front-ends and serverless Workers on Cloudflare, no rented black boxes, no lock-in tax. When the engagement ends, the work is yours — keys, code, and all.

Article III

Keys live on the server. Always.

An API key in the browser is a leak with a countdown. Every model call we wire up runs through a Worker that holds the secret — the client never sees it. Privacy and security aren't a tier; they're the floor.

Article IV

Fast beats heavy

No bundler when a single file will do. Our whole studio site is zero-build and loads instantly; our games run at 60 FPS in a browser tab. Performance is a feature your users feel before they read a word.

Article V

AI is a tool, not a personality

We use AI where it earns its place — drafting, scoping, automating the busywork — and we say so plainly. No mysticism, no "sentient assistant" theater. A good agent is judged by what it does for you, not by how human it pretends to be.

Article VI

Originality, end to end

Every pixel and every sound in our games is original or procedurally generated — no scraped assets, no copyright roulette. What we make for you is yours, clean, and defensible.

Article VII

Counter-positioning

The big agencies sell process; the AI hype-men sell vibes. We sell shipped software that proves itself on the page you're reading. If a competitor can demo it but not deploy it, that's the gap we live in.

Article VIII

This document can change

We'd rather be correct than consistent. When we learn something that makes the work better, we amend the doctrine and date it — out in the open, not quietly. Hold us to the current version.

Like how we think?

The fastest way to test it is to talk to the concierge on our home page — or just tell us what you're building.
