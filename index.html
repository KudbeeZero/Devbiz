Behind the build

Jun 2026

Phase 1 vertical slice ships

Level 1, full moveset, two weapons, three enemy types, the Hive Sentinel mini-boss, and the 7-layer parallax engine — all running at 60 FPS in-browser.

Milestone
Jun 2026

Asset pipeline built

A manifest-driven swap system means AI-generated or hand-painted art can replace the procedural placeholders with zero code changes.

Tech
Jun 2026

Cinematic camera online

Trauma-based screen shake, look-ahead follow, and a dynamic boss-reveal zoom bring the world to life.
